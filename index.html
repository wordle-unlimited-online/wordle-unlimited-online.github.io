What Is Wordle Unlimited?

Wordle Unlimited is an extended version of the popular Wordle game. The rules remain the same. You get six chances to guess a hidden five-letter word. Green means the letter is correct and in the right place. Yellow means the letter is correct but in the wrong place. Gray means the letter is not in the word at all.

The main difference is that you can play as many puzzles as you want without a daily limit. This makes Wordle Unlimited a top choice for players searching for long-tail terms such as "free unlimited Wordle puzzles", "Wordle game with no daily limit", and "Wordle Unlimited online no download".
